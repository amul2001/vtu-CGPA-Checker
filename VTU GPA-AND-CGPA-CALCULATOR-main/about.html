Hello! I am Amul Andure

Who am i?

I am a student who is currently pursuing B.E in Information Science and Engineering (ISE) from the batch of 2020-2024 at Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College.Bidar (VTU university), . With a passion for simplifying the academic journey, embarked on this project together.

Why We Built This Website?

I understand the challenges that students face when it comes to calculating their GPA and CGPA. Many online tools are available, but they often require entering subject credit details and total semester credits, which can be time-consuming and cumbersome.

To overcome this issue, I have decided to build our website. My goal was to create a user-friendly tool that enables students to calculate their GPA and CGPA without the hassle of entering credit details.

What Makes Us Different?

  • Preloaded Courses: My website currently features courses available at VTU University,. I have eliminated the need for students to manually enter course details.
  • Ease of Use: With just a few clicks, students can select their regulation, course, semester, and grades to calculate their GPA and CGPA.
